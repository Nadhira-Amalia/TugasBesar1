About Us

Who we are

Bookie Publishing Company is an independent publisher of books in Indonesia. Headquartered in Bandung, Bookie has been operating since 2015. Our capabilities include title acquisition and development, editorial, design, promotion, sales, warehousing, and distribution.

Bookie has a wide range of title in fiction and non-fiction, including bisnis, young adult, hystory, romance, mystery, cookbooks, health and wellness, animals, design, and more. Bookie continues to expand their titles by acquiring innovative content from existing and debut authors. Bookie books are currently being sold in over 50 cities & continuing to grow. We are partnering with authors to help create stories and communicate ideas that inform, entertain, and inspire, and to connect them with readers everywhere. Our dedicated team of publishing professionals is committed to helping authors realize their very best work and to finding innovative new ways of bringing stories and ideas to audiences.
